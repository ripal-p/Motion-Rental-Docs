# Proof of Identity (POI) Submission

## TL;DR
To verify your identity and complete your scooter subscription, we require documents that confirm your name, address, and bank account ownership. You can often meet these requirements using just one or two documents. This Doc outlines the types of Proof of Identity we accept and how to submit them easily.

## Overview
We require all customers to submit valid Proof of Identity (POI) documents before starting a scooter subscription. These help us confirm your identity, your place of residence, and that the bank account you use is genuinely yours. This ensures safety, accuracy, and compliance with our verification process.

**Common documents include:**

- Utility bills (Electricity, Gas, Internet)  
- Lease or Rental Agreement  
- Australian Rider’s Licence showing your current address  

### Medicare Card or International Passport
**Why we ask for this:**  
Government-issued IDs help us verify your identity and comply with legal requirements.

**You can provide either:**

- A valid Medicare Card issued in Australia  
- A valid International Passport with matching personal details  

These documents are used to confirm your name and identity.

### Australian or Overseas Rider’s Licence
**IMPORTANT NOTE:**  
Make sure your licence is valid for the vehicle you’re subscribing to. For engine size of the vehicle, refer to the [QLD motorcycle license](#) guide.

**We accept:**

- Australian Rider’s Licence  
- Overseas Rider’s Licence (must be valid)  

**Important for French Licence Holders:**

- AM Category: Suitable for scooters up to 50cc  
- A1 or A2 Category: Suitable for scooters up to 125cc (including ours)  

Make sure your licence is valid for the type of scooter you plan to use. If you’re unsure which one matches your needs, refer to the [All Vehicles comparison guide](#).

### Proof of Address
**Why we ask for this:**  
This confirms your residential address matches the information provided during sign-up. It's an essential security and compliance step.

**Accepted documents include:**

- Utility bills (electricity, gas, internet)  
- Lease or rental agreement  
- An Australian Rider’s Licence showing your current address  
- Bank Statement  
- Proof of Account / Proof of Balance Document  

### Proof of Bank Account
**Why we ask for this:**  
We verify your bank account ownership before setting up direct debit for subscription payments.

**You can provide one of the following:**

- A **Proof of Balance** document (recommended – it serves dual purposes)  
- A recent **Bank Statement** showing your full name, account number, and recent transactions  

## Simplifying Your Submission
If you’re using an Overseas Rider’s Licence, you can often simplify your submission. A **Proof of Balance** document may already include both your **address** and your **bank account ownership**, so you might not need to upload separate documents.  
Refer to the [Proof of Balance](#) guide for more details.

### How to Submit Your Documents
You can upload your POI documents securely:

- At our workshop when you arrive  
- Alternatively, if you want to reserve the vehicle, you can let us know and we can sign you up over the phone where we will send you a link to upload all the documents  

Please ensure the documents are clear images or PDFs. Our team will review them promptly and keep you updated on your verification status.

## Final Notes
Using the correct documents from the beginning helps speed up your scooter subscription process. If you're unsure which documents to submit or how to get them, contact our support team for guidance.
