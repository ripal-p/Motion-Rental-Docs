# Get Started with Motion Rental Docs

## TL;DR

This Doc helps you get the most out of Motion Rental’s self-service system. It explains how to find answers, submit forms, and get support – all in one place. Check the Docs, use the Forms page, and contact us only if needed.

## Overview

Welcome to Motion Rental’s self-service Docs. Whether you’re just starting or already on the road, this guide shows how to use our Docs and forms to manage your subscription, rental, service, and support needs efficiently.

## Why Use This Doc?

Using the Docs correctly saves time, avoids delays, and improves your overall rental experience.

**Benefits include:**

- **Faster support** — Solve common issues without waiting  
- **More control** — Handle tasks like bookings, reports, and requests yourself  
- **Fewer mistakes** — Know exactly what info you need from the start  
- **Clear guidance** — Understand how your rental, payments, insurance, and servicing work  

## How to Use Our Docs and Forms

### 1. Check the Docs First

Browse or search the Docs before contacting support. Each Doc gives step-by-step guidance written in plain English. Topics include:

- Rental terms  
- Insurance  
- Payments  
- Vehicle maintenance  
- Support and service  

### 2. Use the Forms Page

Need to request or report something? Use the **Forms** — it lists all the available forms in one place. You’ll find forms for:

- Booking a scooter  
- Reporting incidents  
- Requesting documents  
- Submitting service requests  

### 3. Still Need Help?

If the **Docs** and **Forms** don’t cover your issue, use the contact form on our website to reach us directly. We’re here to help when you need extra support.

## What Makes This System Better?

- **Faster turnaround** — Less waiting, more riding  
- **More clarity** — Know what’s expected at each step  
- **Self-service convenience** — Do it on your schedule  
- **Smarter support** — Our team focuses on complex issues, you handle the basics  

## Final Notes

Use this system as your first stop — not your last resort. Most rental, service, and support tasks can be handled faster and more accurately through our Docs and Forms. Bookmark this page and check back whenever you need to take action or look something up.
